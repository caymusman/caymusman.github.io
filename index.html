About

Hi! I'm Caymus Price (he/him), a 22 year old currently living in Seattle, WA. I graduated from Brown University in May of 2020 with a degree in Multimedia and Electronic Music Experiments.

My concentration in college was about designing and programming my own tools for production - a process that helped me run head first into the conclusion that I knew way less about programming than I thought. In the quest to get better, I found webdev, and fell in love.

I'm a big fan of goldfish crackers, beanies, and being slightly above mediocre at video games. Feel free to reach out to me at any of the links below.
